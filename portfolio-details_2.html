VIDEO ANIMATION

PROJECT INFORMATION

  • Category: Animation
  • Media: After Effects

In live events such as concerts, many artists offer an authentic visual experience with visual effects, where sound, lights, and images merge to create a staging. For this project, I decided to experiment with animation-focused visuals that could be used for live events.


For 9 weeks I was working on a self-directed project where I explored the world of animation. For this project, I made a series of experimental animations that are accompanied by music, more specifically I chose to create 2 video animations with existing songs, one with the song “Oxygen” by Martin Garrix & DubVision feat Jordan Grace, and the other with the song “Take my breath” by The Weeknd.
